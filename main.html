Figourmet, since 1994

Figourmet was created in 1994 by a couple of cookers that loved French plates and wanted to share their recipes with the others. Figourmet was at first a pretty small restaurant, located near Le Peletier in Paris. It changed its location in 2005 to become bigger and be able to welcome to more clients and tasters of the French famous plates. Its address is now in Avenue George V See Contact page. The chef has also changed since 2010, and lead his place to his daughter to take care of that restaurant that is now famous for its French specialities.

Reliable products

Our cooking has one single rule: homemade. That is why the products we select are fresh and come from the market or small local producers. Even our bread is homemade!
